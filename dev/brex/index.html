Brex

Brex is a constant source of challenges and opportunity. During my time I have worked on two teams:

Budgets

Initially, I joined the budgets team to help launch the Empower spend management product. During that time I implemented many core features under the mentorship of the senior engineers on the team. Some of these features included: notifications, internal tooling, feature toggles, performance improvements and more. I also helped to onboard new engineers and define team process, which opened the door for me to get promoted to senior engineer and join the travel team.

Travel

On the Travel team I have been driving many key features of the Brex Travel experience. As one can imagine, travel is an extremely deep product with so many moving parts that must work together to create a great experience for the customer. As a senior engineer on the travel team, I define implementation for much larger features and take success into my own hands on behalf of the team. I get to define product requirements and lead implementation of multiple projects at once by breaking down projects into smaller deliverables and paralleling work amongst myself and my peers. Although the saga is just beginning, I am looking forward to the opportunities that lie ahead
